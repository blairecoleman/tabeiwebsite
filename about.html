About Tabei

Tabei (Tay-Bah-EE)

Junko Tabei, “who stood 5 feet and weighed just 92 pounds, endured rigorous training and conquered treacherous ice and snow in reaching the highest peaks in more than 70 nations.” She was the first woman to ever climb Everest. I named my company in honor of her, because I aim every day to push beyond my limits, and limits in the web design and web development field to produce exceptional final products.

A Note From the President

Hello,
