Initial Meeting

In the our initial meeting, you can expect a casual interview where we get to know your brand and your vision. We discuss the goals you have for your online presence, and even vaguely outline the course of our journey together during this project.

x

Project Planning

The project planning stage consists of a market analysis and an assessment of the materials necessary for your website in order to achieve your online goals. In this phase we discuss you options for your website building platform, and you will receive and estimate. After an approval for your project plan, the first payment is due and your project is truly underway.

x

Wireframing

A wireframe is simply a draft of your web project's design. Depending on what kind of platform we are working with, this comes in many forms. We work together in order to provide a design that is an excellent fit for your brand.

x

Prototyping

This step is entirely dependant on the website build platform. This is simply a more technical and detailed expansion on the winning wireframe design you selected.

x

Development

This phase covers the behind the scene functionality that runs your website and is the muscle underneath the website face. This allows for things like forms, blogs, and subscriptions to actually do the things they are meant to do.

x

Design Construction

After you approve a design and a prototype, the actual structure of your website can begin. That is this phase. The construction of a variety of features and media's come together to shape your project. This is the front end design portion of your website.

x

Walk-through

In this phase, we do a walk-through of your website and analyze all the areas of design and function. At this point we identify any issues with site responsiveness or design and fix them.

x

Web Content

This phase is all about the curating, writing and editing of your website content in order to best represent your brand. This is a valuable part of the process because in our current digitally advanced economic climate, content is king.

x

Publishing

At this point, your project is ready for launch. We handle all the processes to make your website a "live" website.

x

Maintenance

Wrapping up your project, we may discuss different maintenance options based on your website and online branding goals.

Usability and Accessibility

Accessibility is no longer a just "nice" thing to do. It is mandatory and an essential part of shaping the user experience. Your web presence, social media content, and communications need to be accessible, and in line with WCAG 2.0 standards to cater to all of your potential client's needs.
